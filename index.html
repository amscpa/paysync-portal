Configure Cloud Sync (Firebase)

i
Firebase is free from Google. Once set up, your clients submit from their computers and you see it instantly on yours. Takes about 5 minutes.
Step 1 - Create Firebase Project
  1. Go to console.firebase.google.com and sign in with Google
  2. Click Add project, name it "paysync", click through
  3. In your project, click Build > Realtime Database in the left menu
  4. Click Create Database, choose your region, start in Test mode
  5. Copy your Database URL (e.g. https://paysync-xxxxx-default-rtdb.firebaseio.com)
  6. Go to Project Settings (gear icon) > Your apps > click the Web icon (</>) > register app
  7. Copy the apiKey and projectId from the firebaseConfig shown
